# Demo Call Tollgates
Parent: 2.Remembering capturing demand | CLAUDE.md

## Purpose

Tollgates for the demo call.
The same senses orientation as demand-tollgates.md — what did you notice, not what did you analyze.

---

## Before the call — Did I prepare from what the founder already said?

**Check:**
- Read the founder's entry in demo-message-log.md
- Know their exact gap (Job 1), their specific situation (Job 2), their fit question (Job 3)
- Have the Macro → Micro → Nano template ready to run on their real situation

**Do not prepare:** an explanation of the offer, a slide deck, a product walkthrough

---

## Tollgate 1 — Did I start at their gap?

**When:** First 5 minutes of the call
**Question:** Did I open with what they already named, or with an explanation of what I do?

**Signs I started right:**
- Opened with their exact words
- Did not explain mywellnessgame before running the template
- The founder recognised their own situation immediately

**Signs I started wrong:**
- I explained the framework before running it
- I asked discovery questions the founder already answered in messaging
- The conversation felt like a pitch

---

## Tollgate 2 — Did regulation happen?

**When:** During or just after running Macro → Micro → Nano
**Question:** Did the founder feel something shift — or did it stay intellectual?

**Signs regulation happened:**
- Founder paused, went quiet, said "that's exactly it"
- Founder started mapping their own rows without prompting
- The game became visible to them (they named it)

**Signs it stayed intellectual:**
- Founder engaged with the framework as an idea, not a felt experience
- Conversation stayed in explanation mode throughout

---

## Tollgate 3 — What did the close reveal?

**When:** After the call
**Question:** What did the founder's response to the close tell me?

**If they said yes:** Which game was live? What tipped them?
**If they said not yet:** What was missing? Job 1/2/3 not fully done? Or something else?
**If they went silent:** What does the silence tell me?
