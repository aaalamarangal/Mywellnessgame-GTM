# Demo Call
Parent: 1.Remembering finding demand | CLAUDE.md

## What the demo call is

The demo call is Value Delivery.
It is the first live session where Shiva runs the Macro → Micro → Nano template on the founder's real situation.

The close happens when regulation is felt in real time — not when the offer is explained.

---

## What the demo call is NOT

- It is not a pitch call. Never start by explaining mywellnessgame.
- It is not a discovery call. The gap is already named before the call starts.
- It is not a presentation. It is a live demonstration on their specific situation.

---

## How the demo call starts

Start at the gap the founder already named.

Before the call, read the founder's entry in demo-message-log.md:
- What exact gap did they name? (Job 1)
- What specific situation did they tie it to? (Job 2)
- What fit question did they ask? (Job 3)

Open the call with: "You said [exact words from Job 1]. That is where we start."

Never open with an explanation of the offer.

---

## The structure — Macro → Micro → Nano

Run the template live on their real situation.

| Level | What it means | Question to ask |
|---|---|---|
| Macro | What is the big outcome they are building toward? | "What does success look like in the next 90 days?" |
| Micro | What are the key activities that move toward that outcome? | "What specifically needs to happen to get there?" |
| Nano | What are the smallest daily actions inside each activity? | "What does the smallest version of that look like today?" |

Map each Nano row to a game: Clarity / Action / Lesson.
The game that shows up across most rows is where the debt lives.

---

## How the close happens

The close is not a pitch. It is a recognition.

When the founder sees their own situation mapped — Macro to Nano — and can feel the gap in real time, the question becomes: "So what do we do about it?"

That is the close. Shiva names the credit (Clarity / Action / Lesson) that is missing and explains one step forward.

The founder either says yes or they don't. Both are information.
