# 3.Value delivery- remembering demand
Parent: CLAUDE.md

## What this OS is

Value Delivery OS is the third operating system.
It covers the demo call — where regulation is felt in real time.

Value Creation OS and Offer Creation OS both lead here.
Demo commenting (Value Creation) and demo posting (Offer Creation) are the inputs.
The demo call is the output.

## Naming convention

This OS uses **Remembering** (not Recognising).
Recognising = reading the signal for the first time.
Remembering = returning to what the founder already named — their gap, their situation, their fit question.

The demo call starts at what the founder already said. Not at an explanation of the offer.

## Three sub-areas

- **1.Remembering finding demand/** — understanding how the demo call works
- **2.Remembering capturing demand/** — logging what happens in and after each call
- **3.Remembering fulfilling demand/** — patterns from calls that lead to conversion
