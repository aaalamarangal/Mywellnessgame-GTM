# 1.Recognising finding demand

This folder is the finding layer of Value Creation OS.
It answers: where do I find founders who are worth messaging?

## Subfolders

- **1.context/** — frameworks for understanding who the founder is and how to read their signal. Contains Debt-contexts (founder types, business types, situations, debt psychology, coaching diagnostic) and message-contexts (sources, inertia types, signal-bridge-voice pipeline, LinkedIn signal scraping).

- **2.content/** — operational methods for finding founder posts in the field. Currently documents the core sourcing method: Algorithm Feed → Productivity Conversation → Profile Check.

- **3.formula/** — patterns to be built once the content layer shows repeatable structure.

## How this connects to the system

Finding demand is the first step in Value Creation OS.
Before you can capture demand (demo-message-log) or fulfil it (demo commenting), you need to find a signal worth acting on.
This folder is where that starts.
