## Rob Snyder — Demand Framework

**Source:** Rob Snyder
**Core concept:** Coping Pull vs Blocked Pull — demand exists on a spectrum of urgency
**Where it applies:** Step 1 of thinking path — recognising the signal
**How to apply:** Before messaging, ask — is this founder coping or blocked?
**Example in action:** Ross Geiger commenting "Wish this was my usual state" = Blocked Pull. He can't move forward without solving this. Message now.
**Link:** https://howtogrow.substack.com/p/blocked-or-coping

## Yusuf Ahmed — Signal, Bridge, Voice

**Source:** Yusuf Ahmed
**LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/itsyusufahmed/
**Core concept:** Great outreach is a pipeline — Signal → Bridge → Voice. Not an art.
- Signal — find something specific about the prospect
- Bridge — connect the signal to a problem you solve
- Voice — express it naturally with no pitch
**Where it applies:** Steps 1-4 of thinking path
**How to apply:** Before writing — do you have a real signal? If not, don't write yet.
