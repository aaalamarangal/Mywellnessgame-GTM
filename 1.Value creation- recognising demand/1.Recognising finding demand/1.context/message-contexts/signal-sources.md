## 7 Sourcing Methods — Ranked by Reply Rate

All 7 are currently active.

### 1. LinkedIn Algorithm — No Connection
LinkedIn surfaces founders to Shiva based on productivity coaching relevance.
These founders have no engagement with anyone Shiva follows — no likes, no comments.
Completely cold. LinkedIn finds them purely on relevance.
No action required — LinkedIn does the surfacing.

### 2. LinkedIn Algorithm — Warm Network Effect
LinkedIn surfaces founders who have liked or commented on posts from influencers or founders Shiva already follows.
That engagement is what triggers LinkedIn to surface them.
These founders are already warm — they are active in the same circles, even if not yet contacted.

### 3. Existing Founder Profiles — Signal Scan + New Founder Discovery
Shiva intentionally visits existing founders' profiles.
Purpose: find their productivity and regulation signals to message them.
Secondary discovery: find new founders who are engaging in their posts by commenting.
One profile visit can produce both a message opportunity and a new founder to track.

### 4. Complementary and Coaching Influencer List
Shiva has a list of influencers doing complementary coaching work or actual coaching work.
Goes to their posts. Finds relevant founders engaging there.
These founders are active in the coaching and productivity space — already showing interest.

### 5. Inbound DM
Shiva receives a DM directly from a founder.
This comes from content activity or public commenting — not from outreach.
Example: Aannya reached out directly after seeing Shiva's posts.

### 6. Trusted Contact Referral
Someone in Shiva's network runs their own adjacent GTM or content process.
They find a founder's personal productivity posts and share them directly.
Signal comes inbound — no searching required.
Example: Sujith found Tim Scheuer's posts while running his GTM process and shared them.

### 7. Known Event — Find Attending Founders on LinkedIn
Shiva knows a specific event is happening that is relevant to GTM or coaching work.
Finds founders who are attending that event via two routes:
- Event organisers tag them on LinkedIn
- Founders post themselves that they will attend
Shiva goes to their profiles and checks for productivity and regulation signals.
