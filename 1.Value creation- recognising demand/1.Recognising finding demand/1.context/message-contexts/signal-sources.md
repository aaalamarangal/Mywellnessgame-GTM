## 7 Sourcing Methods — Ranked by Reply Rate

All 7 are currently active.

### 1. LinkedIn Algorithm — No Connection
LinkedIn surfaces founders to Shiva based on productivity coaching relevance.
No prior relationship with these founders.
Highest reply rate of all 6 sources.
No action required — LinkedIn does the surfacing.

### 2. LinkedIn Algorithm — Influencer Network Effect
Shiva finds an influencer post and engages with ICP founders commenting there.
Visits their profiles. Checks for personal productivity posts. Engages when it resonates.
Past engagement creates a network signal.
LinkedIn then surfaces founders from that circle automatically — without Shiva having to go looking.
These founders are already warm from prior engagement (even if not logged in Claude).

### 3. DM Through Aannya
Shiva commented and posted publicly.
Aannya reached out directly via DM — organic inbound.
This came from content activity, not outreach.

### 4. Complementary Influencer Posts
Shiva goes to posts from complementary influencers.
Finds founders actively discussing personal productivity in comments.
Engages with them directly.

### 5. Events
Founders presenting demos to investors and customers.
Shiva finds their LinkedIn links from event context.
Checks their profiles for personal productivity posts.
Sends a comment when it resonates.

### 6. Debt Signal Psychology → Google Search
Takes exact lines from debt-signal-psychology.md.
Feeds them into Google search.
Finds founder posts that match that language.
Goes to their LinkedIn and checks for personal productivity posts.

### 7. Trusted Contact Referral
Someone in Shiva's network runs their own adjacent scraping or content process.
They find a founder's personal productivity posts and share them directly.
Signal comes inbound — no searching required.
Example: Sujith found Tim Scheuer's posts while running his GTM process and shared them.
