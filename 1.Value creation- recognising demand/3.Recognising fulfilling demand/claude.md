# 3.Recognising fulfilling demand
Parent: 1.Value creation- recognising demand | CLAUDE.md

## What this folder is

This is the fulfilling layer of Value Creation OS.
It covers demo commenting — 1:1 LinkedIn comments and DMs to individual founders based on their signals.

Demo commenting is the bridge between a founder signal and a demo call request.
It is always a filter. Never a pitch.

## Three sub-folders

- **1.context/** — how demo commenting works, the 3-job filter rule
- **2.content/** — individual demo conversations as they develop
- **3.formula/** — repeatable patterns built from demo conversations over time

## How it connects

- Incoming: signals and interactions logged in 2.Recognising capturing demand/2.content/demo-message-log.md
- Outgoing: leads to 3.Value delivery- remembering demand/ when all three jobs are complete

## The three jobs (summary)

Job 1: Founder names their own gap
Job 2: Founder ties the gap to their current situation
Job 3: Founder asks a fit question

All three must be complete before a demo call is scheduled.
