# Demand Tollgates
Parent: CLAUDE.md | demo-message-log.md

## Purpose
Tollgates are checkpoints that position you to observe what happens after a message is sent.
You have no control over founder responses — only over your messaging at each checkpoint.

The orientation: trust what your senses pick up. Not analyzing the founder from outside — noticing what you sense from inside.

---

## Tollgate 1 — Did I lead with what I sensed?
**When:** Immediately after sending
**Question to ask yourself:** Did I lead with what I sensed or with my solution?
**What to log:** Your honest self-assessment

**Signs I led with my senses:**
- Message was under 100 words
- Referenced their exact words
- Asked one genuine question
- Something in me recognized their experience before I wrote

**Signs I led with my solution:**
- Message explained your framework
- Asked for a call
- Felt like a pitch

---

## Tollgate 2 — What do my senses tell me about the silence or reply?
**When:** 48 hours after sending
**Question to ask yourself:** What do my senses tell me about the silence or reply?
**What to log:** Reply / Silence / Reaction (like/comment)

**If reply:** Move to Tollgate 3
**If silence:** Sub-question — Was it the signal, the translation, or the timing?

---

## Tollgate 3 — Did my senses pick up them going deeper or pulling away?
**When:** When reply comes in
**Question to ask yourself:** Did my senses pick up them going deeper or pulling away?
**What to log:** Their exact words in the reply

**If they went deeper:** Strong signal — continue the conversation
**If they pulled away:** Weak signal — note what didn't land

---

## Tollgate 4 — Has the demo messaging done its job?
**When:** When a founder has replied and a pattern of fit is visible
**Question to ask yourself:** Has my messaging set up the call — or will the call have to do the messaging work?
**What to log:** Job 1 / Job 2 / Job 3 status (see below)

**Three jobs demo messaging must complete before the demo call is worth scheduling:**

| Job | What it means | Indicator |
|---|---|---|
| Job 1 — Gap named | Founder diagnosed themselves. They named what their current system can't do. | Reply names a specific gap, not just curiosity |
| Job 2 — Use case connected | Founder tied the gap to their specific situation right now. | Reply includes a real situation (not "yeah I struggle sometimes") |
| Job 3 — Fit question asked | Founder shifted from "is this relevant?" to "how would this work for me?" | They asked an implementation question — how it fits their workflow, which game to start with |

**If all three complete:** Schedule the demo call. Start at the gap the founder already named.
**If not:** Send one more targeted message. Go deeper into their specific situation. Do not schedule the call yet.

**What demo messaging must never do:** Switch from filtering to explaining.
- Filter = names what is breaking for this founder (invites self-recognition)
- Push = explains what mywellnessgame does (attracts everyone, including the wrong people)
