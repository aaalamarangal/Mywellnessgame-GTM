Parent: 2.Offer creation- Recognising supply | CLAUDE.md

## What this folder is

This is the fulfilling layer of Offer Creation OS.
It covers demo posting — LinkedIn posts that demonstrate the skill publicly.

Demo posting is distinct from demo commenting (Value Creation OS).
Demo commenting is 1:1 and private. Demo posting is public and broadcast.
Both lead to demo calls.

## Three sub-folders

- **1.context/** — what demo posting is, principles for writing demo posts
- **2.content/** — one entry per demo post published
- **3.formula/** — repeatable patterns built from demo posts over time

## How it connects

- Incoming: insights from 1.Recognising finding supply/ and 2.Recognising capturing supply/
- Outgoing: demo posts that attract founders who self-select into a demo call conversation
